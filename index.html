Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our Train Data Project solutions.

What makes Train Data Project different from international options?
Train Data Project offers a critical advantage: data sovereignty. By storing all data exclusively in Canadian data centers, your information never crosses borders or falls under foreign jurisdiction. This ensures compliance with Canadian privacy laws like PIPEDA and protects your data from foreign surveillance laws like the US CLOUD Act.
How does your pricing compare to major cloud providers like AWS, Azure, or GCP?
Our pricing is competitive with major international providers while offering the added value of complete data sovereignty. While AWS, Azure, and GCP may sometimes offer lower base prices, they often include unexpected costs like data transfer fees, zone charges, and currency exchange fluctuations. Our transparent, CAD-denominated pricing provides better budget predictability and often results in 15-30% overall savings for comparable workloads.
How difficult is it to migrate from our current cloud provider to Train Data Project?
Our data repatriation process is designed to be seamless. Our migration specialists work with you to create a tailored migration plan that minimizes disruption. We support standard VM formats, containers, and custom configurations. Most migrations can be completed in stages to avoid downtime, with typical projects completed in 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and data volume.
What support options do you provide?
We offer bilingual (English and French) support through multiple channels: 24/7 phone support for critical issues, email, and ticketing systems for regular questions, and scheduled video consultations for complex matters. All support is provided by Canadian-based technicians who understand local regulations and compliance requirements.
Can you support specialized workloads like machine learning or data analytics?
Yes! Our cloud infrastructure includes GPU-equipped nodes (RTX 4060+) specifically designed for ML/AI workloads. We support popular ML frameworks, containerized applications, and large data processing pipelines. Our high-memory configurations (up to 128GB per node) are ideal for data analytics, and our high-speed network connections between nodes optimize distributed computing performance.
